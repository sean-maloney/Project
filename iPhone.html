Features

The Apple iPhone 14 features a stunning 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display, 5G connectivity, and A14 Bionic chip for blazing-fast performance.

iPhone 14 and iPhone 14 Plus are splash, water and dust resistant, and were tested under controlled laboratory conditions with a rating of IP68 under IEC standard 60529 (maximum depth of 6 metres up to 30 minutes).

Specifications

6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display.

5G connectivity.

A14 Bionic chip.

Dual-camera system with 12MP Ultra Wide and Wide cameras.

Available in 64GB, 128GB, 256GB, and 512GB capacities.
